1. Download and install NSIS from nsis.sourceforge.net
2. Run NSIS compiler
3. Ensure to have opendylan.chm - generated by hhc opendylan.hhp in htmlhelp - in this directory!
4. Adjust APPVERSION in fundev.nsi
5. Run build-installer.bat
6. The installer is output to opendylan-${APPVERSION}-win32.exe
7. (partial) List of known bugs/todos is at the top of fundev.nsi
